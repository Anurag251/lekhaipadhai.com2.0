About Us

We Are Increasing Business Success With Technology

Over 25 years working in IT services developing software applications and mobile apps for clients all over the world.

We are Education based platform having a vision to support learning journey.Main purpose of lekhaipadhai is to help Nepalese students to choose best course according to their capability , interest , location and achieve their core goals in the preferred sector. We are with the best colleges/universities/schools of Nepal which shows their course detail , fee structures, facilities and other extra features clearly which help students to achieve a perfect Education destination. LekhaiPadhai provides proper guidance to go with the best for the healthy educative future to the students. We help to those who are facing problems in education sector and still in delima regarding their further study. Also, LekhaiPadhai engaged in online entrance prepation sector, provides notes and useful exercises and previous year questions for the better learning experience.
